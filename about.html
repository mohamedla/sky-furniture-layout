Our policy

Charge policy

For charge value will be the price of the product plus the delivery feeds as what will be showed on the bill.

The delivery feeds will depend on the type product and the distance from the nearest sky store.

The charge will be throw credit card, on your confirm of the order the bill value will be hold until you confirm the delivery the bill will charge.

Deliver & recover policy

The delivery will be received in a duration form 7 to 15 days.

When you confirm the delivery you confirm that its status and quality as displayed on the site.

In cause of returning the product the request must be in duration of 7 days and we will pick it in duration from 7 to 15 days, and you can recover your money when the product is picked and its status confirmed.

custom order policy

The custom order will be made in or factories and the duration it will depend on the nature of the order and the materials need and the delivery duration.

The duration will be showed when you make the order.
